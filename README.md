# Habit-IQ

A smart habit tracking and intelligence platform designed to help you build better habits and achieve your goals through data-driven insights and personalized recommendations.

## 🎯 Features

- **Habit Tracking**: Log your daily habits and monitor your progress over time
- **Analytics & Insights**: Get detailed analytics on your habit patterns and streaks
- **Goal Setting**: Set SMART goals and track your progress toward them
- **Streak Counter**: Visualize your consistency with intuitive streak tracking
- **Smart Reminders**: Receive timely notifications to stay on track
- **Progress Dashboard**: View your overall progress with beautiful visualizations
- **Habit Categories**: Organize habits by categories (Health, Fitness, Learning, etc.)
- **Data Export**: Export your habit data for personal analysis
